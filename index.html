The Bright Dunce

By Michael Coffman

About the Book

A Vietnam memoir about war, memory, survival, and the long aftermath. This is not a polished myth. It is lived experience, told directly.

About the Author

Michael Coffman is a Vietnam veteran whose writing reflects on service, memory, resilience, and the long arc of a life lived through conflict and reconstruction.
